1. Information we collect

We collect the following categories of information:

a. Information you provide directly

  • Account information: your first and last name, email address, chosen username, public handle/tag, date of birth (used to verify you meet the minimum age), and the password you create (stored in hashed form by our authentication provider; we do not have access to your plaintext password).
  • Vehicle and build data: the vehicles you add (make, model, year, trim, mileage, and specifications) and the maintenance, repair, and modification entries you log, including titles, categories, dates, costs, part and shop names, notes, and goals or budgets you set.
  • Photos and media: images you choose to attach to log entries or your garage, and any metadata contained in those files.
  • User-generated content: comments and any other content you submit in fields visible within the app.
  • Communications: information you provide when you contact us for support, including the contents of your messages.

b. Information collected automatically

  • Approximate location: if and only if you grant permission, we use your device's location to find nearby shops that can perform a selected modification and to provide directions. We use this location at the time of your request and do not continuously track or store a history of your movements.
  • Device and usage data: technical information necessary to operate the Service reliably, such as device type, operating system, app version, general diagnostic and crash information, and interactions needed to deliver features.
  • Local storage: the app stores data on your device (for example, a local cache of your garage) so the app works offline and loads quickly.

c. Information from third parties

  • Payment and subscription status from Apple and our subscription management provider (we receive confirmation of your subscription/entitlement status; we do not receive your full payment card number).
  • Authentication events from our authentication provider.

2. How we collect it

We collect information directly from you when you create an account, add vehicles or entries, upload photos, submit comments, or contact us; automatically through your use of the Service and your device (subject to permissions you grant); and from third-party providers who help us operate the Service, as described above.

3. How we use your information

  • To create, maintain, authenticate, and secure your account.
  • To provide, personalize, and sync core features — your build log, cost tracking, service-interval reminders, goal- and budget-based modification recommendations, nearby-shop search, PDF history export, and public garage sharing (where you choose to enable it).
  • To generate AI-assisted modification recommendations based on the vehicle and goal details you provide (see Section 4).
  • To process and manage subscription purchases and your CarBox Pro entitlement.
  • To respond to your support requests and communicate with you about the Service.
  • To monitor, maintain, debug, and improve the Service, and to ensure security and prevent fraud, abuse, and violations of our Terms.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our agreements.

4. Artificial intelligence & automated processing

CarBox uses artificial intelligence to power certain features. When you request modification recommendations, details about your vehicle (such as make, model, year, trim, specifications, your selected goal, and budget) are transmitted to our third-party AI provider, Anthropic, PBC, which processes that information to generate suggested modifications and related descriptions. We do not send your name, email address, password, or contact details to the AI provider for this purpose.

Important disclosures about AI-generated content:

  • Recommendations, descriptions, price and performance estimates, and other AI-generated outputs are produced automatically by a machine-learning model. They are provided for general informational purposes only, may be inaccurate, incomplete, outdated, or unsuitable for your specific vehicle, and are not reviewed by a human before you see them.
  • AI outputs do not constitute professional mechanical, engineering, safety, legal, financial, or emissions/regulatory advice. You should independently verify any recommendation with a qualified professional before acting on it.
  • We do not use AI to make decisions that produce legal or similarly significant effects about you without your involvement.
  • Service-interval reminders, vehicle specifications, distances, ratings, and shop information are likewise estimates or third-party data and may not be accurate for your situation.

Your continued use of AI-powered features constitutes your acknowledgment of these limitations. Please also review the corresponding disclaimers in our Terms of Service.

5. How we share your information

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not share it for cross-context behavioral advertising. We disclose information only as follows:

  • Service providers that process data on our behalf under contract and only to operate the Service (see Section 6).
  • Public garage sharing: if you choose to make a garage page public, the vehicle, specifications, log entries, and photos on that page become viewable by anyone who has the link. Do not include information you wish to keep private on a page you make public.
  • Legal and safety: when we believe disclosure is reasonably necessary to comply with a law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request; to enforce our Terms; or to protect the rights, property, or safety of CarBox, our users, or others.
  • Business transfers: in connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, reorganization, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction, subject to this Policy.
  • With your consent or at your direction.
